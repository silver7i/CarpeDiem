February 26th: A Refreshing Reset
Today was one of those days where I finally felt like I was getting back into the groove. I started my morning with a huge cup of iced coffee—classic me—and actually had a bit of time to just sit and breathe before the chaos of work started. Usually, I'm running around like a headless chicken, but today felt different.

Work was pretty productive for a change. I finally knocked out that project that’s been hanging over my head all week. My boss even gave me a quick "good job" in the hallway, which honestly made my day. It’s funny how a tiny bit of validation can totally shift your mood.

After work, I hit the gym. I wasn’t really feeling it at first, but once I got my playlist going, I caught a second wind. Now, I’m just winding down on the couch, catching up on some Netflix. I’m feeling pretty content. Hopefully, I can keep this momentum going for the rest of the week. Finger crossed!
